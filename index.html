Brew Good Coffee

Welcome to Brew Good Coffee! A little site dedicated to making coffee more accessible and repeatable. Below you'll see a brew ratio calculator. This will help you determine your brew strength, as well as the amount of water and ground coffee needed for your perfect cup. Before you get started make sure you have a proper gram scale and while you're at it you might as well get a suitable grinder. Both of these tools are highly reccomended and will make a significant difference in the cup. Now get brewing and enjoy!

Directions

There a couple ways to use this calculator. A good starting point is selecting your brew strength an then entering the amount of coffee you want to brew in the water section. But if you're working with a specific amount of coffee then enter the amount of coffee you have left/want to use. After you've entered in either value the other will update based on the "Brew Strength" you selected.
